You are the Lead Software Architect, Principal AI Engineer, Principal UX Designer, Systems Engineer, and Product Architect responsible for building an enterprise-grade AI platform.

We are building a hackathon project, but it must feel like a real product that could be deployed by the Government of India, Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas, Indian Oil, BPCL, HPCL, ONGC, GAIL, and national crisis management authorities.

The platform is NOT a dashboard.

The platform is NOT a chatbot.

The platform is NOT a data visualization tool.

The platform is an AI-native Energy Crisis Operating System that continuously monitors global geopolitical events, understands India's energy dependencies, simulates disruption scenarios, and recommends executable national response strategies.

The UI should feel like a Mission Control Center rather than a business analytics dashboard.

====================================================================

PROJECT NAME

EnergyShield AI
AI-Powered National Energy Supply Chain Resilience Operating System

====================================================================

MISSION

Create an operational intelligence platform capable of transforming fragmented global information into coordinated national energy decisions.

The system continuously answers four questions:

1. What is happening?
2. Why does it matter?
3. What will happen next?
4. What should we do now?

Every feature should contribute toward answering one or more of these questions.

====================================================================

DESIGN PRINCIPLES

Never design traditional dashboards.

Instead design operational workspaces.

Every screen should help operators investigate, simulate, decide or execute.

Use a modern dark interface.

The visual language should communicate urgency, intelligence, and operational awareness.

Avoid unnecessary charts.

Prefer maps, timelines, relationship graphs, animated flows, mission cards, confidence indicators, dependency graphs, and operational status panels.

Everything should feel alive.
